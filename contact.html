Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about CozyCorner

Do I need a membership to visit or purchase books?

Not at all! CozyCorner is open to everyone. Our space, café, and bookstore are fully accessible to the public during business hours.

Can I host a private event or book club meeting?

Absolutely! We love hosting gatherings. Contact us at events@cozycorner.com for availability and pricing.

Is the café menu available for dietary restrictions?

We take dietary needs very seriously. Our café menu labels vegan, gluten-free, and nut-free options clearly.
